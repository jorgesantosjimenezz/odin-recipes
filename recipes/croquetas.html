Croquetas

Description:
Typical dish from Spain. One of the greatest meal you can try.

Ingredients: -Ham -Butter -Flour -Milk -Eggs -Bread crumbs -Olive oil

Steps:
1. Make Roux: Melt butter, add flour, stir, then slowly whisk in milk until creamy. Add ham.
2. Chill: Let mixture cool, then refrigerate until firm.
3. Shape & Coat: Form small logs, dip in egg, then coat with bread crumbs.
4. Fry & Serve: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown, drain, and enjoy.
