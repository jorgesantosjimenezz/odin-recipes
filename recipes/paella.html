Paella

Description:
Typical dish from Valencia, Spain. One of the greatest meal you can try.

Ingredients: -Tomato -Rice -Prawns -Pepper -Salt -Olive oil -Onion -Garlic -Chicken

Steps:
1. Prep & Sauté: Heat olive oil in a large pan, sauté chopped onion, garlic, and bell pepper until soft. Add chicken or seafood and cook lightly.
2. Add Flavor: Stir in diced tomatoes, paprika, and saffron (or turmeric as substitute).
3. Add Rice & Broth: Pour in short-grain rice and chicken/seafood broth. Don't stir once spread evenly.
4. Cook & Serve: Simmer until rice absorbs liquid (about 20 min). Add peas or shrimp on top, rest a few minutes, then serve.
